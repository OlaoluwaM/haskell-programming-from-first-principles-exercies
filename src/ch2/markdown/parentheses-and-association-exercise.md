# Parentheses and association

1. The result will be altered by the parenthesis
   1. $8 + 7 * 9 = 71$
   2. $(8 + 7) * 9 = 135$
2. The results will remain the same between the functions
   1. `perimeter 3 4 == 14`
   2. `perimeter2 3 4 === 14`
3. The results will be altered by the parenthesis
   1. `f 4 == 11`
   2. `f2 4 == 0.36363636`
